Privacy Policy

Effective date: 10 March 2026  ·  Last updated: 10 March 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Graid ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the Graid mobile application and website at graidapp.co.uk.

1. Who we are

Graid is a UK-based educational app designed to help GCSE students revise through AI-generated flashcards. Our website is graidapp.co.uk.

2. Information we collect

We collect the following information when you use Graid:

  • Account information — your email address and password when you register
  • Profile information — your name, year group, and subjects (student accounts)
  • Usage data — flashcards studied, quiz scores, streaks, and session activity
  • Device information — device type, operating system, and app version for troubleshooting
  • Payment information — subscription payments are handled by Apple or Google. We do not store your card details.

We do not collect sensitive personal data, location data, or data from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent.

3. How we use your information

  • To provide and improve the Graid app and its features
  • To personalise your revision experience
  • To send you weekly progress digests (parent accounts, where enabled)
  • To manage your subscription and process payments
  • To respond to support requests

We do not sell your data to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising.

4. Parent & child accounts

Graid supports linked parent and student accounts. Parents can view their child's revision progress. Students can accept or decline parent link requests at any time. We take the privacy of young people seriously and collect only what is necessary to provide the service.

5. Third-party services

Graid uses the following third-party services to operate:

  • Supabase — secure database and authentication
  • Anthropic (Claude API) — AI-generated flashcard content
  • RevenueCat — subscription management
  • Resend — transactional email (weekly digests)

Each of these services has their own privacy policy. We only share data with them as necessary to provide the Graid service.

6. Data retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at the address below.

7. Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You also have the right to object to processing or request a copy of your data in a portable format. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us.

8. Security

We use industry-standard security practices to protect your data, including encrypted connections (HTTPS) and secure authentication. No system is completely secure, but we take reasonable steps to protect your information.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via the app or by email.
